History of the font

Science Gothic is a libre sans serif variable font designed originally by Morris Fuller Benton. Science Gothic however, is a team effort. Thomas Phinney, Vassil Kateliev and Brandon Buerkle all took part in its primary development. This versatile form is used for a wide range of purposes. From business and technology to makeup to superheroes and detectives, this font takes on stunning and elegant designs. In addition to adding a lowercase by the founders, Science Gothic incorporates four design axes which are extreme weight and width, plus contrast and slant.
